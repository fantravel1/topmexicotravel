Quick Tips for First-Time Visitors

Entry Requirements

Most nationalities enter visa-free for up to 180 days. Bring your passport and keep your FMM tourist permit safe.

Money Matters

Mexico uses the Peso (MXN). Cards are widely accepted, but carry cash for markets and small businesses. Tip 15-20%.

Stay Safe

Tourist areas are very safe. Use Uber instead of street taxis, don't flash valuables, and trust your instincts.

Best Value

Eat where locals eat for amazing food at low prices. The midday "comida corrida" meal deal is unbeatable value.
