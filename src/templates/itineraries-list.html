Trip Planning Tips

Best Time to Visit

November to April offers the best weather across most of Mexico. Avoid major holiday weeks for lower prices.

Booking in Advance

Book popular restaurants and experiences 2-4 weeks ahead. Flights and hotels are cheapest 6-8 weeks out.

Getting Around

Internal flights save time; ADO buses are comfortable and affordable; car rentals offer flexibility for beaches and ruins.
